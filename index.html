SMF Parking Pulse

See how Sacramento Airport parking availability changes before you head to SMF. Refreshed about every 10 minutes.

Live Updated|

Capacity Assumptions

The airport previously published each lot's total number of spaces on its parking page at https://flysmf.gov/to-and-from/parking. This dashboard uses those totals to estimate occupied spaces by subtracting the currently reported free spaces from each lot's total spaces.

Total Available Spaces

Calculated by adding each lot's reported free spaces at each parking update. Lots with no free-space value are excluded from this sum.
